Becoming a Cyborg

Neuro_Hacker is a 10 minutes game DEMO, as my final year thesis, aimed at enhancing the immersive game experience through real-time facial capture. The game was made in Unreal Engine 5, and the real-time player facial expression data obtained from Live Link Face and was uploaded to the Unreal Engine 5 blueprint. These data in game was used to simulate the player's sensory system in cyber space. In this game, the player will become a Neural Hacker hacking in the cyber space in someone's mind. The firewall that block player is manifested as some kind of human body-part-like object in this space, and the player needs to hack in to the firewall by connecting their own consciousness to control the firewall to break a security hole to pass.

During the game exploration process, when the player closes their left eye, they can activate the spatial scanning mode, and the screen enters post-processing rendering mode, displaying interactive and highlighted items. When hacking the surveillance camera, player needs to turn their head to control the orientation of the camera. This project is an experiment in immersive gaming experience, creating new modes of interaction between people and screens.

Human Machine

In today's society, electronic devices have become an increasingly indispensable part of people's daily lives. People are communicating their thoughts, emotions, and lives through an electronic box like never before. People's thoughts are transformed into electronic signals, and printed as thousands of the gratings on the screen. On the other hand, it can also be said that electronic devices have grown fresh flesh and blood, becoming containers for humans - hiding scattered fragments of a person.
